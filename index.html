Home

Welcome! I'm Vedran, an 18-year-old high school student from Macedonia, and this is my personal homepage. Programming became my interest afer finishing primary school when I discovered the "Python Crash Course" by Eric Matthes and I've been doing it ever since. I've had my fair share of hiccups along my journey but I finally feel like I am starting to get the point. Being young still, I want to explore all areas of programming. I've dedicated most of my time to back-end development mainly working with Python (Flask/FastAPI).

In addition to programming, I'm interested in Linux too. First I started with ubuntu and after messing with some other distributions as well I have decided to stick with arch, btw ;). My current setup consists of Arch and i3. I find the workflow much better compared to Windows since you're in control of your environment. All of my dotfiles are hosted at my Github.

Outside of the tech world I enjoy racing my bicycle, currently riding for my local club Velo-M, and also enjoy reading and playing piano.
